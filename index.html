Documents needed for registration

A valid photocopy of your passport or identity card is required, along with a recent medical report. These documents are essential for the registration process and must be presented before starting the course.

Prices

The full program for category B costs 32,000 ALL, covering theory and practice fees at the Transport Directorate. A theory-only option is available for 25,000 ALL. The fee also includes access to video lessons, test simulations, and installment payment options.

Time

Registration deadlines and exam dates are strictly set. The theory exam must be taken within 15 days of registration and is valid for six months. The practical exam must follow within 25 days and is valid for one year after the theory exam date.

Course schedules

Lessons are offered at 09:00–10:30 and 18:00–19:30, both in person and online. Practical training sessions are scheduled flexibly, according to each student’s availability, ensuring comfort and convenience for all learners.
